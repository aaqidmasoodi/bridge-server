Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a translation issue?

If you notice incorrect translations, please email us at feedback@bridgechat.live with details about the text that was mistranslated.

Can I request a new language?

Absolutely! We're constantly adding new languages. Email your language requests to ideas@bridgechat.live.

Is my conversation private?

Yes, all conversations are temporary and automatically deleted when the chat ends. We don't store any chat data.

How accurate is the translation?

We use state-of-the-art AI models that provide highly accurate translations for most common phrases and sentences.
